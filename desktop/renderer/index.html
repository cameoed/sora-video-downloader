Manual Connect

Problems logging in? You can paste your bearer key directly in the text field below to connect instead.

To retrieve your Sora bearer key, visit your Sora profile in any browser, right-click and Inspect the page, click Network tab, refresh the browser, look for the row called "me", click it, click Headers, scroll down to find the wall of text called "Bearer abc123etc" and copy that entire value into the text field below!

Draft Backup

Cookie + bearer needed!

In order to save your drafts watermark free, you need to visit Sora in your browser and copy both the raw Cookie header and the matching Bearer Bearer Authorization from the same network request, pasting them below.
